What 1,500 GCC workers told us

The GCC workforce is unlike anywhere else on earth — majority expatriate, rapidly transforming, and navigating AI change faster than policy can follow. Wave 01 reveals what's really happening inside these workplaces.

🌍

A Workforce Unlike Any Other

Majority expatriate. Rapid transformation. For the first time, we have rigorous, regionally-specific data on how people inside GCC workplaces are actually doing.

1,500+ Voices

Behind the GCC People Pulse

Co-Founder, EXE: Employee Experience Exchange

Lorna Cole is co-founder of EXE: Employee Experience Exchange, a research and insight consultancy working with organisations across the GCC. Alongside co-founder Ruth, Lorna leads the GCC People Pulse — the region's first monthly workforce sentiment tracker — developed in partnership with Ipsos in the Middle East.
