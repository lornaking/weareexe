What 1,500 GCC workers told us

The GCC workforce is unlike anywhere else on earth — majority expatriate, rapidly transforming, and navigating AI change faster than policy can follow. Wave 01 reveals what's really happening inside these workplaces.

About the research

Behind the GCC People Pulse

LC

Lorna Cole

Co-Founder, EXE: Employee Experience Exchange

Lorna is a workplace culture and communications expert with 15 years of experience in the Middle East. Since 2012, she has helped organisations including Cleveland Clinic Abu Dhabi, ADNOC and UAE government entities create purpose-driven cultures where people thrive. An ICF-certified coach, Lorna combines expertise in strategy, storytelling, facilitation and leadership development to translate strategy and values into intentional employee experiences.

RD

Ruth Dance

Co-Founder, EXE: Employee Experience Exchange

Ruth is a respected voice in workplace culture and experience, with a track record of helping leaders make employee experience a strategic driver of performance. She has partnered with major brands including BP, Co-op and Aviva, guiding organisations through complex change with clarity, empathy and commercial rigour. Ruth is known for bridging the gap between customer and employee experience with ambition and execution.
