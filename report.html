Why this matters

A workforce unlike any other

For the first time, organisations across the GCC have access to a dedicated, data-driven view of what workers across the region actually think, feel and experience at work. The GCC People Pulse: The Word on Work is the region's first monthly workforce sentiment tracker, providing a regular measure of employee sentiment across the region.

The GCC workforce is unlike anywhere else in the world. Rapid transformation, evolving workforce expectations, nationalisation priorities, and the growing impact of AI are reshaping the workplace.

Yet until now, there has been no consistent, region-specific measure of workforce sentiment across the GCC.
